Live Sports Everyday

If you love bikes – and you’re looking for a memorable holiday that leaves you not just a fitter, but also a smarter cyclist – then this exclusive camp at the UCI World Cycling Centre in Aigle, Switzerland is going to blow you away. Founded in 2002, the UCI World Cycling Centre (WCC) is a training centre for promising athletes from developing nations. With alumni like Chris Froome, it’s no wonder that more than 100 athletes a year, across Road, BMX racing, MTB and Track cycling, live and train at the WCC. This once in a lifetime trip takes you behind-the-scenes of this incredible centre providing unprecedented access to the facility, staff, coaches and riders.
